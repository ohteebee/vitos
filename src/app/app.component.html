About Us

It's not just about the food, it's about the experience. From the time you first walk in the door, you are part of the family. We want our guests to feel at home when here, we go the extra mile to get you what you need. Service with smile.

Homestyle authentic italian food right in your backyard. Owner Pat Leccese, sons Vito, Joey and Michael, and wife Lori all have a key role in the business. It's always been family first. From New York, to Maryland, to Greenville, to Maryland, back to the Upstate in Greer.
